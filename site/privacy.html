Privacy Policy

BookLaxDriver collects booking details only to respond to inquiries, quote trips, coordinate service, process secure payments through third-party providers, and keep customer records.

Information we may collect

Name, phone number, email address, trip date and time, flight number, pickup and drop-off locations, passenger count, luggage count, vehicle preference, and special service requests.

Payments

BookLaxDriver does not ask customers to send card numbers through WhatsApp, SMS, email, or this website. Card details should be entered only on the secure hosted payment page from Stripe, Square, or another approved payment provider.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact BookLaxDriver through the official booking channel shown on this website.
