Service Terms

All rides are subject to availability, route confirmation, vehicle fit, price confirmation, and written booking confirmation.

Booking

A booking is not confirmed until trip details, price, terms, and any required deposit/payment are confirmed in writing by BookLaxDriver.

Payment

Deposits or balances may be requested through a secure payment link or invoice. Do not send card numbers through chat, SMS, email, or website forms.

Airport pickup, waiting time, and cancellation

Airport pickup method, waiting grace periods, cancellation windows, no-show rules, overtime, parking, tolls, and extra stops must be confirmed before the ride. Final published terms should match BookLaxDriver's approved business policy.

Vehicle and luggage

Vehicle capacity depends on passenger count, luggage count, child seats, and route needs. BookLaxDriver may recommend a different vehicle if the requested vehicle is not suitable or available.
